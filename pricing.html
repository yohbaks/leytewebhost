Absolutely! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Upgrades are instant, and we'll prorate the difference.

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all plans. If you're not satisfied, we'll refund your payment—no questions asked.

Yes! For larger organizations with specific requirements, we offer custom infrastructure, dedicated servers, and white-label solutions. Contact our sales team to discuss your needs.

Pro and Enterprise plans include free assisted migration. Our team will handle the entire process to ensure a seamless transition with zero downtime.
